Omicron BA.2.12 needs to be watched for spread, say experts

22 April, 2020

PUNE: New Omicron sub-variant BA.2.12 is more transmissible than BA.2 and will need to be closely monitored for spread and hospitalisation rates, top public health experts told TOI on Thursday. The experts said the surveillance data will be key as there's limited information on BA.2.12's severity.Sources from INSACOG - India's network of surveillance labs - had claimed that Omicron derivative BA.2.12.1 has also been found in a few samples from Delhi, but these reports remain unconfirmed.
